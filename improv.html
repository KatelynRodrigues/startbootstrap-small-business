Improv

In order to keep our brains from being overloaded, we take periodic breaks as a group. During these breaks we play group games that are designed to help us get to know each other and have fun.

Some of these game include:

  • Ninja
  • Crazy Eights
  • Zip, Zap, Zop
  • Hawk, Knife, Spaghetti
  • Mind Meld
  • Evolution
  • WAH
  • Stretch and Share
  • Game of Threes
  • Sculptures

Our breaks take anywhere from 15-30 minutes.
